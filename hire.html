About Hiring
About Candidates

Are any costs involved to provide job-ready talent for hire?

No, you can hire for absolutely zero-cost. Moreover we offer you a hassle-free experience throughout the process with a dedicated Interview Success Expert from our end.

What are the roles Nxtwave Hire can help our company fill?

Our candidates would be suitable for a variety of roles like
- Frontend || JavaScript, ReactJS
- Full Stack || MERN
- Software Developer
- Web UI Developer || HTML, CSS Flexbox
and more...

What is the hiring process?

1. Get in touch with our Hiring Expert and define your requirements
2. Receive profiles in 24-48 hours
3. Start your interview process with the candidates
4. Onboard the candidates in less than a week
‍
Link to get in touch with our Hiring Expert - https://forms.ccbp.in/public/form/hire-with-nxtwave

Is the hiring process online or offline?

We suggest that the entire hiring process be online to ensure maximum candidate participation

Can I get sample profiles?

You can find sample profiles here

What is your turnaround time? How soon I can get profiles?

You would receive profiles in 24-48 hours(working days) once the hiring expert receives the clear requirements.

How long will it take to hire a developer from NxtWave?

You can onboard a candidate in less than a week. A clear job description and quick interview turnarounds can further reduce this duration.

Can I hire software developers for full-time or part-time jobs?

Our developers are available for full-time opportunities. If you're looking to hire on a part-time basis, please get in touch with us here.

In what technologies are candidates trained in?

Our candidates are trained in:
• HTML, CSS, Bootstrap
• Javascript
• React JS
• Python
• SQL
• Node JS
• Express JS
• Test Automation || Unit & Integration and much more...

Who are your candidates? How long are your candidates trained?

The majority of our candidates are fresher graduates who have been trained for 8 months on the technologies like Frontend Essentials(HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap), React.js, Python, SQL, QA, etc.

Do you assess or verify the candidates skill?

We verify and vet candidates through rigorous assessments, mock tests, mock interviews, and more. The process ensures that companies receive apt profiles to conduct the interview process.

Will you train candidates on the skills that we require?

We can discuss custom training requests in detail based on your requirements.
To raise a request, you can fill out this form
‍Our hiring expert will reach out to you.

Are these candidates ready to join immediately?

Yes, our candidates are willing to join immediately after selection.

Which locations are your candidates willing to work?

Our candidates hail from across the country. They're open to working from the majority of the locations in India. Currently, our candidates are working in 25+ cities.

What is the probability of a candidate joining us if given an offer?

More than 97% of our candidates have joined the companies from which they received their first offer.
